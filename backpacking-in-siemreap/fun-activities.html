Useful Tips for Travelling Enjoy the best that the city can offer

The best way to travel around and visit different temples is by Tuk Tuk (Motor taxi). You can rent it for a day trip that would usually cost around $15-$20. The motor taxi driver will be able to take you to all the good places and make good use of your time.

Delicious Treats, Good Eats Street foods paradise

One of the highlight of touring this town is its mazing foods with cheap price. There are many good restaurant just out side of the Angkor temple where you can get foods and a quick snacks with affordable price

It is more fun under the sun Jungle Excursion

If you are done with the temple and wanted to find something fun and exciting to do, zip lining could be the answer. It is a world-class adventure that is thrilling, while keeping an emphasis on safety & fun. This adventure will take you through the Angkor's jungle for more sight seeing.

Biking Tour Get closer by bike

Seeing more of the temple and getting to know more about the Angkor Wat with the local biking tour group. It is a great opportunity for any backpacker to make new friends and to get to know the surrounding area.

Stay on budget Travel with less

You can save a lot of money by staying at a hostels that is usually close by the town area. Many of the hostels for backpacker have breakfast included. And to get around , you rent a bike or take tuk tuk to go to places.
