Cambodian Cultural Village Get to know the culture more

Cambodian Cultural Village is a theme park and museum in Siem Reap, Cambodia. It is about 6km from the town and located on the road from the airport.

Special Performances There are eleven unique villages, representing varied culture heritage of nineteen ethnic group. One of the special highlight is the main tradional Cambodian style called "Apsara dancing". Beside this, there are performances of ethnic minorities from the north-east of Cambodia, traditional wedding ceremony, Khmer circus, peacock dancing, elephant show and much more.
