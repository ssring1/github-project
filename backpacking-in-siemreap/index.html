Exciting Journey Worth Experience to Share

Watch the sunrise at the Angkor Wat and explore the Angkor Wat and the surrounding area then watch the sunset at Phnom Bakheng.You can rent a bicycle or motorbike to tour around the city and end your long day with the Pub Street in Siem Reap.

There are so many guest houses and hostels in the area of Angkor where you can find amazing prices with breakfast included. Many of this places offer bike rental with really cheap price and will help you find a rental car if you need to go out of the area for more exloring.

Experience Home Cook Meals The Life of the local

Learning how to cook Khmer cuisine with the local. It is a really fun activity that you can join while making new friends and meet other people from all around the world that have the same interet and passion like you do.

You can join a tour to the village to learn how to farm rice on the rice field and experience how the local live their life and do their work on the daily basic.
